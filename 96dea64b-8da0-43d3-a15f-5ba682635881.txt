Energen Corporation (NYSE: EGN) senior managers will meet with members of the investment community at the Deutsche Bank Energy One-on-One Corporate Days in Boston on September 29-30, 2015. Associated meeting materials will be available for viewing on Energen's home page, http://www.energen.com, beginning Tuesday, September 29, at 1:00 p.m. ET. Energen Corporation is an oil and gas exploration and production company with headquarters in Birmingham, Alabama. At year-end 2014, the company had 1.1 billion barrels of oil-equivalent proved, probable, and possible reserves and another 2.2 billion barrels of oil-equivalent contingent resources. These all-domestic reserves and resources are located primarily in the Permian Basin in west Texas. For more information, go to http://www.energen.com.
